Short Quote

Long Quote

"People know I am a Muslim, but my religion is humanity, which I believe is the greatest religion in the world. When my ambulance goes out to save a life, it does not ask if the person bleeding on the road is a Muslim, a Christian, a Hindu, or a Sikh. It only sees a human being in pain."
