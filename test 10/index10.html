The Nutrients

Proteins, carbohydrates, and fats form the foundation of everything you eat. Instead of cutting out entire food groups, the key to lasting energy is balancing your plate. Carbohydrates provide quick fuel, proteins rebuild muscle tissue, and healthy fats support cell growth and hormone production.

Aim to fill half your plate with colorful vegetables, one-quarter with lean protein, and one-quarter with complex carbohydrates like quinoa or brown rice.

Read the official dietary guidelines on the Nutrition.gov Website.
